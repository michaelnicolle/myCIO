Why myCIO

Three things no one else is doing together.

Senior enough to set direction. Accountable enough to see it through. Honest enough to tell you when something is not working, including when it is us.

We started myCIO because we kept seeing good businesses get let down by their own technology.

Not through bad luck. Through a model that was never built to deliver outcomes. Providers who keep the lights on, bill their hours, and walk away. The business owner gets left holding the decisions nobody else will own.

What was missing was someone in the room. Not reporting from a distance. Not selling at the end. Present, accountable, and on your side of the table.

So we built the missing piece.

01

We do not leave until it is working.

Most consultants deliver a report and move on. We own the outcome and fix what breaks. You stop being the person explaining to the board why it did not land.

02

Your vendors finally have someone to answer to.

We hold them accountable, renegotiate what is not working, and close the gaps they have been ignoring. One client saved half a million dollars in the first review. That is not unusual.

03

Built so you are never held hostage.

We build your environment so it belongs to you. Documented, portable, and not dependent on any single vendor or person. Seven years from now, it should still make sense.
