What we do

From the foundations up to where growth happens.

Six service domains, grouped into three layers. The foundations everything else depends on. The strategy and governance running through every decision. And the layer at the top where business growth happens.

The six domains

Strategy at the top. Plumbing at the bottom.

01

Technology strategy and governance.

The work that sets direction for everything else. We own the roadmap, hold vendors accountable, and make sure the board gets clean answers instead of guesses. This is where most engagements start.

02

Data and business knowledge.

Your business knows things. The question is where that knowledge lives. We help you capture what runs the business, structure it properly, and build the foundation AI actually needs to work.

03

AI and automation.

Most businesses are stuck using AI as a tool individuals visit. We help you work out where you are on the journey and what it takes to get to AI running as a layer through the business. Then we make it happen.

04

Microsoft 365 and modern work.

Most businesses are paying for M365 and using a fraction of what it offers. We fix that. Proper configuration, licensing that reflects what you actually need, and the modern work capabilities that turn M365 into the place work gets done.

05

Hybrid cloud and infrastructure.

On-premise, in the cloud, or somewhere in between. We start with what the business needs, then design the environment that fits. Right workload, right platform, right cost. Not lifted from a template.

06

Security and networking.

Security runs through every decision we make and every environment we touch. Network architecture that fits how your business operates, zero trust done properly, and a security posture you can stand behind. Clean board answers. Cyber insurance readiness. No gaps for an auditor or an attacker to find.

How we engage

Three ways of working with us.

01

Fixed-fee engagement

A scoped piece of work with a clear outcome. Used in the early stages where the priority is getting an honest picture and fixing the foundations. Priced before any commitment.

02

Monthly retainer

Ongoing technology strategy and governance advisory, embedded in the business. Used once the foundations are in place and the focus shifts to standardising, transforming, and keeping the business protected. The core of how we work.

03

Day-rate consulting

Defined ad hoc tasks or specialist input. Used when a client needs something outside their retainer scope. Quoted by the day, not the hour.

Every engagement starts with a structured discovery. You get a clear picture of your situation and a recommended path forward, including cost, before any longer commitment is discussed. If it is not the right fit, we will say so.
