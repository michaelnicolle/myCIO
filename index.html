Questions you might have

A few things people ask us

Do you replace our existing IT team or MSP?

Not always. For Strategy and Governance we sit above what you already have and make sure it is heading somewhere sensible. For Your Technology Team we take full ownership and use trusted local partners where it makes sense. We are not here to add headcount for its own sake.

How do you charge?

Strategy and Governance is a fixed monthly retainer. Your Technology Team is a single monthly fee covering strategy, governance, security, vendor management, and delivery. No additional labour costs, no PO requests. For large projects or on-site work outside Auckland we agree the approach up front.

Are we too small or too big?

If you are big enough to feel real pain from technology that does not quite work, but not big enough for a full-time CIO and internal team, you are likely a good fit. We typically work with businesses spending $5,000 or more per month on technology.

What does a first engagement look like?

A straight conversation, no slides, no jargon. We listen, ask a few direct questions, and give you a plain-language view of where you stand. From there we agree a clear first piece of work so you see value quickly without a long programme commitment.
