Questions people ask us

A few things worth knowing.

Do you replace our existing IT team or MSP?

Not always. For Strategy and Governance we sit above what you already have. Your existing team or MSP keeps doing what they do, and we make sure it is heading somewhere sensible. For Your Technology Team we take full ownership of the outcome and use trusted local partners where on-site work is needed. We are not here to add headcount for its own sake.

How does pricing work?

Strategy and Governance is a fixed monthly retainer. Your Technology Team is a single monthly fee covering strategy, governance, security, vendor management, and delivery. No additional labour costs, no PO requests on top. For large projects or on-site work outside Auckland, we agree the approach up front before any work begins. No surprises.

Are we the right size?

If you are big enough to feel real pain from technology that does not quite work, but not big enough for a full-time CIO and internal team, you are likely a good fit. We typically work with businesses spending $5,000 or more per month on technology across their tools, vendors, and managed services.

What does a first engagement look like?

A straight conversation: no slides, no jargon, no prep needed on your end. We listen, ask a few direct questions, and give you a plain-language view of where you stand. From there we agree on a clear first piece of work, so you see real value quickly without signing up for a long programme.

What if we need on-site support outside Auckland?

We have a long-standing network of trusted local partners across New Zealand. For anything requiring hands-on work outside Auckland, we bring in people we know well and have worked with before. You deal with us. We deal with them. The accountability stays with myCIO.
