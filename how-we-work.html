How we work

From where you are, to where the business is going.

Five stages. By the end, the business runs on something documented, defensible, and built to last.

01 Discover Weeks 1–2
Outcome
You know exactly what is broken, what is at risk, and what to fix first. No guesswork. No vendor spin.
What happens
Full environment review. Every risk, gap, and priority documented. No assumptions. No upsell agenda. You get a written report with our honest read on where things stand and a clear recommendation on what to do next. No obligation to go further.
02 Stabilise Months 1–2
Outcome
The fires are out. Your team stops working around technology and starts working with it.
What happens
Fix the foundations. Reduce technical debt and risk. End the recurring breakdowns. We hold existing vendors accountable and close the gaps that are costing you time and money every week.
03 Standardise Months 2–4
Outcome
Your business no longer depends on who happens to be in the room. The technology is documented, defensible, and anyone can pick it up.
What happens
Right people, process, and technology at the right service levels. Everything documented. Vendor relationships structured properly. The business can answer any question a board or auditor might ask.
04 Transform Month 4+
Outcome
Your technology is actively working for the business. Your team is faster, your board is confident, and you are not the bottleneck.
What happens
Modern work, AI, and automation built to last. Real competitive advantage, not a pilot project. Every initiative tied to a business outcome and measured against it.
05 Secure Ongoing
Outcome
Clean board answers. A safe environment. A security posture you can stand behind.
What happens
Continuous threat detection, compliance, and resilience. Security baked into every decision, not bolted on at the end. Risk reported clearly to the board. No jargon, no surprises.
How we engage

Three ways of working with us.

01

Fixed-fee engagement

A scoped piece of work with a clear outcome. Used in the early stages where the priority is getting an honest picture and fixing the foundations. Priced before any commitment.

02

Monthly retainer

Ongoing technology strategy and governance advisory, embedded in the business. Used once the foundations are in place and the focus shifts to standardising, transforming, and keeping the business protected. The core of how we work.

03

Day-rate consulting

Defined ad hoc tasks or specialist input. Used when a client needs something outside their retainer scope. Quoted by the day, not the hour.

Every engagement starts with a structured discovery. You get a clear picture of your situation and a recommended path forward, including cost, before any longer commitment is discussed. If it's not the right fit, we'll say so.

Where we start

Every engagement starts the same way.

A 30-minute conversation. No prep needed. You'll leave with a clear picture of where your technology actually stands. If there's something worth doing together, we'll tell you. If there isn't, you'll still know more than you did.

From there, we run a structured discovery. You get a clear picture of your situation and a recommended path forward, including cost, before any longer commitment is discussed.
